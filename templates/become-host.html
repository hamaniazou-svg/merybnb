Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list my property? ▼

Listing your property is completely free! We only earn when you do. We take a 15% commission on each booking.

When will I get paid? ▼

Payments are processed within 48 hours after guest check-out. You can withdraw to your bank account anytime.

What if a guest damages my property? ▼

All properties are covered by our Host Protection Plan. You can file a damage claim up to 30 days after checkout.

Do I need to host full-time? ▼

No! You control your availability. Host whenever you want or just when you need extra income.
